## How to get them to me (pick one)

- **Easiest:** export from Apple Photos (File → Export → JPEG, "Full Size"), drop the files in this folder.
- **From the Mini/AirDrop:** AirDrop to the MacBook, move into this folder.
- Don't worry about size/format — iPhone HEIC is fine, I'll convert + compress.
